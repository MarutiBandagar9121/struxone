ABOUT  STRUXONE

Global Capability Centers (GCCs) have evolved from operational support units to strategic powerhouses driving digital transformation and innovation. While only 27% of Forbes 2000 companies have established their centers in India, this presents a tremendous opportunity for growth and competitive advantage.

At Struxone, we partner with world-leading organizations to unlock the full potential of global talent in their transformation journey. We enable businesses to strategically build, manage, and scale high-impact global capability hubs that deliver measurable value.

What sets us apart is our comprehensive, end-to-end solution suite – encompassing advisory, talent acquisition, workspace optimization, and business enablement services. We serve as the essential partner for forward-thinking enterprises seeking digital excellence and access to premium talent to accelerate their strategic objectives.
