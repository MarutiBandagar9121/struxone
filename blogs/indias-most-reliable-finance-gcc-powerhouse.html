India's Most Reliable Finance GCC Powerhouse

Shammi Prabhakar Published on: November 30, 2025

Pune Is Quietly Becoming India’s Most Reliable Finance GCC Powerhouse Walk through Pune’s talent corridors today — Kharadi, Viman Nagar, Baner, SB Road, Bavdhan — and you’ll see a pattern that’s hard to ignore.

Global Capability Centers are leaning heavily on Pune to scale their Finance, Controllership, FP&A, Tax, Audit, and Treasury operations.

What makes this shift compelling is the sheer diversity of Finance footprints now concentrated in the city:

Core Tech & Digital Finance Hubs

  • Oracle (Bavdhan)
  • Coupa (SB Road)
  • Eaton (Kharadi)
  • NIQ (Kharadi)
  • Workday (Viman Nagar)
  • TomTom (Yerwada)

Cyber & Engineering GCCs Adding Finance Pods

  • SecurityHQ (Baner)
  • Kohler (Baner)
  • Regal Rexnord (Balewadi)
  • Cummins (Kothrud / Balewadi)
  • Atlas Copco (Talegaon / Dapodi)
  • Linde (Hadapsar)
  • Air Products (Viman Nagar)

And now, the strongest pull — large global banks/BFSI players.

Pune is increasingly a preferred node for Finance Ops, Regulatory Reporting, Risk, Audit, Legal Entity Control, and Financial Analytics for:

  • Citi
  • JPMorgan
  • Credit Suisse
  • UBS
  • BNY Mellon
  • Barclays
  • Mastercard
  • TransUnion
  • Experian
  • CRIF
  • S&P Global
  • MSCI
  • Nasdaq

This clustering isn’t random.

It’s a strategy.

Leaders are choosing Pune for one core reason: A dependable, multi-domain Finance talent ecosystem that can scale quickly and operate with global standards.

From FP&A to SOX, from Product Costing to Regulatory Reporting, the city now delivers end-to-end capability — and does it consistently.

As global enterprises rethink their Finance operating models for 2025–2030, Pune’s value proposition is becoming clearer:

  • Strong finance domain depth
  • Experienced leadership pools
  • Intersections with analytics, digital, and risk
  • High stability and low attrition pockets

Reliable talent supply in Kharadi–Viman Nagar–Baner belt If you’re evaluating India for scaling Finance, Pune is no longer the “second choice.”

It’s quietly becoming the most future-ready Finance GCC cluster in the country.
