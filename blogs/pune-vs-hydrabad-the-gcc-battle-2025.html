The Scale Story (2025 Status)

Hyderabad: The "Speed Scaler." Home to ~355+ GCCs. It captured 40% of all new GCC setups in India over the last 3 years. It is built for massive headcount ramp-ups (3,000+ seats).

Pune: The "Quiet Giant." Surprisingly ahead with ~360+ operational GCCs. It is the second most preferred destination globally for capability centers, driven by a recent surge in flexible workspace leasing (up 91% YoY in Q1 2025).
