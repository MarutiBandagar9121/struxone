Maharashtra Governments Progressive GCC Policies

StruxOne Published on: November 15, 2025

The Government is paying you back for every dollar you spend on your GCC.

The Government of Maharashtra has just rolled out one of the most progressive GCC policies yet — designed to reward global enterprises investing in innovation, analytics, finance, and technology talent in the state.

What’s changing:

For the first time, a policy doesn’t just promise infrastructure — it actually pays back a portion of what you spend on:

  • Office space
  • Salaries
  • Innovation & R&D

It's promising single window approval (yet to pass the reality test) and several non-financial benefits as well.

And here’s the interesting part — these incentives aren’t limited to new setups only.

Even existing GCCs expanding their operations in Maharashtra are eligible for several of these benefits.

That means your growth story now comes with a built-in financial boost.

Early insight from our ongoing research:

The framework is de-risking setup and expansion costs like never before. Tier-2 cities such as Nagpur, Nashik, and Sambhajinagar are being developed as new GCC growth corridors with greater benefits (yes, talent and local ecosystem is a question mark yet).

Whether you’re planning a new GCC or scaling an existing one, Maharashtra is signalling that it’s ready to co-invest in your success.

This isn’t just policy support — it’s partnership economics.

We are meeting policy makers and compiling a concise, data-backed policy summary and comparative brief as part of a new research initiative.

Bottom Line

Maharashtra is signaling that it’s ready to co-invest in your success.
